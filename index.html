About Paul VI

A Legacy of Faith & Excellence

For nearly four decades, Paul VI High School has been shaping the minds and character of Basotho youth.

Paul VI High School was established in 1986 in Taung, Mohale's Hoek, as a Catholic secondary school committed to providing quality education rooted in Christian values. Over the years, the school has grown into a beacon of academic excellence and character formation in the region.

Our vision is to be a center of holistic education that produces disciplined, God-fearing, and responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to the development of Lesotho and the world.

Our mission is to provide a conducive learning environment that nurtures intellectual growth, moral integrity, and practical skills, empowering every student to reach their full potential.

Boarding Life

Home Away From Home

Our boarding facilities provide a safe, nurturing environment where students can focus on their studies and personal growth.

Boys' Boarding

A structured and supportive environment for boys, with dedicated housemasters, study sessions, and recreational activities.

  • Comfortable dormitories
  • Supervised study time
  • Recreation and sports
  • 24/7 pastoral care

Girls' Boarding

A safe and empowering space for girls, with experienced matrons, academic support, and a strong sense of community.

  • Secure and comfortable rooms
  • Academic mentorship
  • Life skills development
  • Health and wellness support

Both boarding houses are supervised by qualified staff, ensuring a safe and conducive learning environment for all students.
